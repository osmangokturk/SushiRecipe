A SUSHI RECIPE FOR YOURSELF!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 3 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 5 sheets nori
  • 200g fresh salmon
  • 1 avocado

Preparation

Cook the sushi rice and season it with vinegar, sugar, and salt. Lay the nori on a bamboo mat, spread rice evenly, and add sliced salmon and avocado. Roll tightly and slice into bite-sized pieces. Serve fresh and enjoy!
